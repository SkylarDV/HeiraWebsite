Your Safety, Our Concern

Heira's Development

3 students working together

Three students are collaboratively working on the same core concept for their bachelor’s project. While the underlying idea and objectives of the project are shared, each student will independently design and develop their own version of the safety application. This approach allows for individual implementation, creativity, and technical decision-making, while maintaining a common conceptual foundation across all projects.

The Designs

So far development of the route planner and audio recorder have been started

The route planner will ensure you make it home with the shortest, safest route and that you always have a place that welcomes you in case of emergency

The audio recorder will collect evidence of any unfortunate encounter if those were to come to pass, giving you a headstart in opening a case

Designs are subject to change as app development progresses.
